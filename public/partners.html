The Terms

Plainly stated, so there is nothing to read between the lines.

No Exclusivity

We invite a small panel of specialists to each vertical — never a single point of failure, never a lock-in. The partner who responds fastest and quotes sharpest earns the flow. No fees either way.

The Money Stays Yours

Client funds move on your regulated paper — MYBA escrow, broker trust, your own accounts. caléna never custodies money. We verify and reconcile; we do not hold.

The Member Stays Ours

You never see the customer until the sale is approved. Briefs arrive anonymised; identity is shared at confirmation, under NDA, with a non-solicit on introduced members.

Performance, Not Tenancy

This is a referral relationship, not a directory listing. We ask for a committed response SLA, named cover when your principal is away, and evidence of insurance and client-funds handling. Standard hygiene — our members are why.
